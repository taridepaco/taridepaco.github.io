About me

Great curiosity has moved me all my life. First, I was fascinated by planes and why they flew, so I studied Aerospace engineering. Then I was amazed with researching, so I decided to take a Ph.D. in propulsion systems. During this time I discovered my passion with automatizing processes and coding, so now I am looking forward to applying the problem-solving approach I have developed during these latest years to real industry needs.

During the Ph.D. period followed by two post-doc years, important cross skills have been acquired, such as teamwork abilities, autonomy to manage and expose results of research project in collaboration with industry and thinking outside the box. Finally, software tools in which I have specialized may be highlighted. Concerning 3D modelling and Fluid simulation, Autodesk Inventor and Siemens STAR-CCM+ have been widely used. To automatize processes and script physical and stochastic models, object-oriented programming languages Matlab and Python have been thoroughly learned.
